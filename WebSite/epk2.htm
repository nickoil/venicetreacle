Venice Treacle – “Bad Aji”

A Quank-driven pop nugget about man versus machine

Out 12 December 2025

Listen (For Press Only)

Pre-release stream – please do not share publicly.

Bad Aji takes inspiration from legendary Go master Lee Sedol and his historic defeat by DeepMind’s AlphaGo. Venice Treacle reinterpret the confrontation through their Quank aesthetic: wonky rhythmic jolts and melodic fragments mirror the shifting dynamics of Sedol’s battle, an early engagement in an enduring war which the algorithms seem to be winning. Composed, performed, and mixed entirely by humans, with just a dash of AI sauce in the mastering.

Venice Treacle, known for their unpredictable Quank-driven alt-pop, blend wonky grooves with melodic bursts. Their debut EP, Modern Elixir, was played in its entirety by BBC Introducing.
